Life

Life is interesting.

  • There will be a time when you feel, it's over.
  • You are not created here in this earth just to give up your life.
  • Don't over-blame yourself for the mistakes around you or yourself.
  • We humans learn through mistakes.
  • Always keep in mind that time will heal you eventually.

Don't be too overwhelmed by anything in this world.

  • You can't win this world but you can help yourself understand where you stand.
  • I made a lot of mistakes in my life, but I never give up to learn or be better.
  • Otherwise, there won't be any hope left in this world.

Always be content

  • Remember there is no such a thing as I have enough in this world.
  • So be content of what you have or what you are granted by God.
  • You don't know what will happen tomorrow.
  • Until today, there is no service where we can raise dead people.

Wisdom, Knowledge, Understanding

  • Knowledge is power but without wisdom that knowledge itself is a danger to you.
  • Understanding is the one of the most important requirements we should have as a human being.
  • The truth shall set you free.
  • If you feel like your life is worthless, reconsider what you believe or change what you have come to believe and find another path.
